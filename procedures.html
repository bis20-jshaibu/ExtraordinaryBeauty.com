steps to follow
when wearing makeup

Beautiful skin requires commitment not a miracle

Follow the following steps to do your makeup beautifully.

1.Start with moisturizer to hydrate your skin.

2.Apply your ptimer.
Primer helps to keep your makeup on espacially on warm days.

3.Then apply your favourite type of foundation.
You can apply using foundation brush or your fingers.

4.put concealer where you want more coverage.
Use a brush to blend in since it gives full coverage makeup look.

5.Apply bronzer to add back somecolor to your face.

6.Highlight the high points.

7.Define your eyes with eyeliner.

8.You can a swipe on mascara to curl your eye lashes.

9.Line your lips using lip liner.

10.paint on your lipstick and lip gloss

11.Then set up your makeup by using your setting powder and spray.
